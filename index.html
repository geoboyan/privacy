1. Introduction SafeStep ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles information, specifically regarding Location Data and SMS services. SafeStep is designed as a personal safety tool and operates under the principle of Data Minimization. 2. No Server-Side Storage (Zero-Server Architecture) SafeStep is a "local-only" application. We do not own or operate any external servers to store your personal data. Your contacts, emergency numbers, and custom messages are stored locally on your device’s encrypted storage. We have no access to your data, and we cannot sell or share it with third parties. 3. Location Data (GPS) SafeStep requests access to your device's location (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION). Purpose: To generate a Google Maps link containing your coordinates during a manually triggered SOS event. Usage: The location is processed only at the moment you confirm the SOS. Storage: Coordinates are not logged or stored. Once the SMS is sent, the coordinate data is cleared from the app’s active memory. 4. SMS Services (SEND_SMS) SafeStep requires the SEND_SMS permission to function as an emergency tool. Usage: The app sends an SMS only when you manually confirm the "Send SMS" prompt. Costs: SMS messages are sent via your mobile network provider. Standard carrier rates or roaming charges apply. SafeStep does not collect any fees for sending these messages. 5. Third-Party Services (Advertising) SafeStep displays a horizontal ad banner provided by [e.g., Google AdMob]. These third-party providers may use device identifiers to serve ads. You can manage ad personalization in your Android System Settings. 6.
