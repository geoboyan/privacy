PRIVACY POLICY: COSMIC UNTANGLE Last Updated: February 22, 2026 INTRODUCTION Cosmic Untangle is a mobile application developed by geoboyan Technologies (Georg Grichtmaier), based in Munich, Germany. This app is a puzzle game designed for entertainment. We value your privacy and aim for full transparency regarding data usage. ZERO DATA COLLECTION (LOCAL-ONLY) Cosmic Untangle does not collect, store, or transmit any personal user data to our own servers. Game Progress: Your high scores, reached levels, and settings are stored locally on your device via Android SharedPreferences. No Registration: No email address, name, or phone number is required to play. DEVICE PERMISSIONS The app requires the following technical permissions to function: Haptic Feedback/Vibration: Used for game effects and structural integrity alerts. Audio Settings: Used to play the background music and sound effects. THIRD-PARTY ADVERTISING This app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. To provide this service, Google AdMob may collect and use certain data, including: Your device's Advertising ID. General location data (Country/City level). App interaction data (ad views and clicks). This data is processed by Google and is used to show relevant ads. You can manage or reset your Advertising ID in your Android system settings under "Google" -> "Ads". CHILDREN'S PRIVACY Cosmic Untangle is a general audience game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Our ad providers are instructed to follow industry-standard safety regulations for younger audiences.
